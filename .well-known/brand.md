# Encoded Brands

Encoded Brands is a consultancy and protocol company that helps marketers, agencies, and platforms operationalize brand identity for AI agents. We liberate brand standards from static PDFs and platform-specific walled gardens, turning them into version-controlled, portable, machine-readable protocols. 

We serve marketers who refuse to let their agents ship poor content, platforms (like Shopify, Meta, and Canva) that want to offer seamless brand onboarding, and cross-functional teams that need a single source of truth for brand governance. By moving brand identity into a centralized schema, we ensure that every agent inside a company's stack, across its vendors, and beyond its control can faithfully express and protect the brand.
